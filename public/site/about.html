/ ABOUT

Money should move on its own.

Most people do not have time to manage their money every day. They have lives, jobs, and families to send to. RemitRoute is an autonomous agent that handles the recurring parts of personal finance for everyday people across Africa, so the routine just happens.

/ THE FRICTION TODAY
Remittances are a chore

Sending money home means remembering the date, opening an app, and pushing the transfer every single time.

Savings sit idle

Stablecoins in a wallet earn nothing. Moving them into yield is a step most people never get around to.

FX is manual

Holding the right mix of local and dollar currency takes constant attention that nobody has time for.

/ THE IDEA

Set one rule. The agent does the rest, forever.

You tell RemitRoute what you want in plain language. It runs onchain on a heartbeat, paying gas in stablecoins so you never touch CELO, and stamps every action with a proof you can check. You stay in control: funds are yours, caps are live, and you can withdraw any time.
